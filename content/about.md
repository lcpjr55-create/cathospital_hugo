## ​Veterinary care for cats only   


![Picture](/images/7/5/8/2/75820791/img-1161_orig.jpg)

The Cat Hospital of Durham and Chapel Hill is a full-service veterinary clinic that cares exclusively for felines. We have been caring for cats here in the triangle in a calm and friendly environment for over fifteen years. From the kitten to the senior cat, we are here to help with all of your cat's medical needs with the highest standards of veterinary care.  
  
We offer all levels of care from exams and vaccinations to surgery and dental procedures. We also offer emergency care for cats of all ages.  
  
We offer boading for our patients. Our peaceful boarding area has condos for each kitty. Our staff takes pride in working with the cats to make them feel comfortable and loved while boarding with us.
