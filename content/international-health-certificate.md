## International Health Certificate  


![Picture](/images/7/5/8/2/75820791/unnamed-4_orig.jpg)   
  
  
  
  


* * *

|  ​If you plan to leave the continental United States with your pet, you will need an international health certificate. Our doctors are authorized to issue these certificates. Each country's travel requirements are different, and as many as six to twelve months of preparation may be required. You can read more by clicking the link below. [Click here for more information about international health certificates](https://www.aphis.usda.gov/aphis/pet-travel/pet-travel-info-and-guidance-document/steps-to-obtain-intl-health-certificate)
