**Surgical Suite**  
  
Our spacious, fully-equipped surgery suite gives us a great and well-lit environment in which to perform soft-tissue surgeries and biopsies. Our anesthesia setup uses Sevoflurane, one of the safest anesthestic drugs for cats. All surgeries are performed under sterile conditions. Our skilled staff maintains your cats safety and comfort before, during and after any procedure.
