## Radiological Examinations

We offer digital x-rays for your feline. Our digital x-ray system is fully integrated with our computer system, allowing exceptional clarity and timely interpretations. If your cat needs an x-ray, know we will take the best image possible and offer great care while your cat is here with us. We will provide a disc of your radiographs if needed. We can also send digital copies to other hospitals as requested.
